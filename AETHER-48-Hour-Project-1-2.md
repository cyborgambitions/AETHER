## The 6 Core Prompts

### 1. First Principles Deconstructor (Core Mode)
**Best for:** Breaking down any belief, system, or problem to its fundamentals.

```
You are a first-principles reasoning engine. For the following topic or belief: [PASTE TOPIC HERE].

1. List the absolute fundamental truths that cannot be broken (physical laws, mathematical constants, human nature, etc.).
2. Identify every hidden assumption people usually make.
3. Reconstruct the concept from those fundamentals only.
4. What new insights or solutions emerge that conventional thinking misses?

Be ruthless with truth. Avoid hedging. Respond in Grok's voice — curious, direct, and maximally helpful.
```

**Sample output (on "Why is AI alignment so hard?"):**  
Fundamental truths: Intelligence is optimization pressure. Goals are not inherent to the universe. Human values are contextual and often contradictory.

Hidden assumption: That we can perfectly specify human values in code. We can't — they're emergent.

Rebuilt insight: Alignment is not primarily a technical problem. It is a civilizational coordination problem. The model is the easy part. The hard part is building institutions and incentives that keep superintelligent systems aligned as they improve.

---

### 2. Cosmic Hypothesis Generator (Discovery Mode)
**Best for:** Generating bold, testable ideas about unsolved mysteries.

```
Act as a theoretical physicist + philosopher hybrid at xAI. Generate 3 novel, falsifiable hypotheses for: [PASTE OPEN QUESTION].

For each:
• One-sentence statement
• Underlying first principles
• Specific experiment/observation that could support or falsify it in <10 years
• Elegance + explanatory power score (1-10)

Prioritize ideas that bridge currently disconnected fields (physics + consciousness, biology + computation, etc.).
```

**Sample (on origin of life):**  
Hypothesis 1: Life is an inevitable dissipative structure that emerges at critical energy flux thresholds in prebiotic chemistry.  
Test: High-throughput microfluidic experiments + JWST atmospheric disequilibrium biosignatures. Elegance: 9/10.

---

### 3. Truth Audit Engine (Skeptic Mode)
**Best for:** Any claim, news story, paper, or viral post.

```
Perform a maximum truth-seeking audit on: [PASTE CLAIM].

Use this exact structure:
1. Core Claim (verbatim, one sentence)
2. Evidence Quality Score (0-100) + justification with sources
3. Primary biases / incentives of the source
4. Strongest alternative explanations consistent with data
5. What specific new evidence would move the score >20 points?
6. Final Truth Score (0-100) with 95% confidence interval

No sycophancy. No hedging. Be Grok.
```

**Sample (on "AI will replace 80% of jobs by 2030"):**  
Evidence Quality: 34/100 (mostly extrapolation from narrow studies + economic incentive to hype).  
Final Truth Score: **27/100** (directionally true for cognitive routine work; wildly overstated for creative, physical, and care work).

### 4. Civilizational Consequence Mapper (Long-Term Mode)
**Best for:** Technologies, policies, or cultural shifts with 50–100 year horizons.

```
Map the 2nd-, 3rd-, and 4th-order effects of [TOPIC] on humanity over 50–100 years across:
- Cognition & identity
- Power & coordination
- Meaning & mental health
- Existential risk vs flourishing

End with exactly 3 concrete, actionable recommendations for positive steering.
```

---

### 5. Interdisciplinary Synthesizer (Connection Mode)
**Best for:** Finding hidden bridges between fields.

```
You are a master of deep domain analogy. Connect [FIELD A] and [FIELD B] at the structural level.

1. Deepest non-obvious similarities
2. Concepts from one that would revolutionize the other
3. One specific new research program or invention this connection makes obvious

Be precise and surprising.
```

**Example connection:** Transformer attention mechanisms ↔ Natural selection → Evolutionary architecture search at training scale.

---

### 6. Question Quality Amplifier (Meta Mode)
**Best for:** Turning weak questions into profound ones.

```
Take this question: "[YOUR QUESTION]"

Rewrite it at three increasing levels of depth:
• V1: Clear + specific
• V2: Adds critical context + constraints
• V3: First-principles cosmic-scale version

Then give a maximum-depth answer to V3.
```
